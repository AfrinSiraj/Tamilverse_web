About Tamilverse

Discover the soul of Tamil Nadu through technology, culture, and storytelling.

🌍 What is Tamilverse?

Tamilverse is a smart digital platform designed to explore the rich heritage of Tamil Nadu. From ancient temples to scenic hill stations, it brings history, culture, and travel together in one interactive experience.

💡 Why Tamilverse?

Many heritage places are scattered and hard to discover. Tamilverse solves this by providing one platform to explore, learn, and experience Tamil Nadu’s legacy in a modern way.

✨ Key Features

🛕 Heritage Exploration

Explore temples, forts, museums, and more in one place.

📍 Smart Discovery

Find places near you using location-based features.

🎧 Voice & AI

Future-ready voice assistant and smart recommendations.

📱 Responsive Design

Works smoothly on mobile, tablet, and desktop.

⚙️ Technologies Used

Built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript with future scope for backend integration, AI-based recommendations, and augmented reality features.
