Adi Kumbeswarar Temple – Kumbakonam

Adi Kumbeswarar Temple is one of the most ancient and spiritually significant temples in Tamil Nadu.

Dedicated to Lord Shiva, worshipped here as Adi Kumbeswarar, the temple is deeply associated with Hindu cosmology.

The temple showcases magnificent Dravidian architecture with intricately carved mandapams and towering gopurams.

The Mahamaham festival, celebrated once every twelve years, attracts millions of devotees.
