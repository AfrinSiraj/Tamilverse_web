Airavatesvara Temple – Darasuram

Airavatesvara Temple is a historic temple located in Darasuram near Kumbakonam, Tamil Nadu.

It was built by the Chola dynasty in the 12th century and is dedicated to Lord Shiva.

The temple is famous for its exquisite stone carvings, detailed sculptures, and unique chariot-shaped mandapa.

It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a remarkable example of Chola architecture.

With its artistic beauty and historical importance, the Airavatesvara Temple attracts visitors from all over the world.
