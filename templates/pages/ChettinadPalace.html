Chettinad Palace – Karaikudi

Chettinad Palace is a magnificent heritage mansion located in Karaikudi, Tamil Nadu.

It showcases the rich culture and lifestyle of the Chettiar community.

The palace is famous for its grand architecture, teak wood interiors, and antique decorations.

It reflects a blend of Indian and European architectural styles.

Visitors are amazed by its elegance, heritage, and timeless beauty.
