Chidambaram Nataraja Temple – Chidambaram

Chidambaram Nataraja Temple is a famous and ancient temple located in Chidambaram, Tamil Nadu.

It is dedicated to Lord Nataraja, the cosmic dancer form of Lord Shiva.

The temple is renowned for its unique architecture, sacred Chidambara Rahasyam, and beautiful golden roof.

It is one of the Pancha Bhoota Sthalams, representing the element of space (Akasha).

With its deep spiritual meaning and rich cultural heritage, the temple attracts devotees and tourists from across the world.
