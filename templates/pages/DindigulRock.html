Dindigul Rock Fort – Dindigul

Dindigul Rock Fort is a historic hilltop fort located in Dindigul district of Tamil Nadu. It stands atop a massive rock rising about 900 feet above the surrounding plains.

The fort has been ruled by various powers including the Madurai Nayaks, Hyder Ali, Tipu Sultan, and the British, making it historically rich and strategically important.

Known for its steep climb and strong defensive walls, the fort offers breathtaking panoramic views of Dindigul town and nearby landscapes.

Today, Dindigul Rock Fort is a protected monument and a popular tourist attraction for history lovers and trekkers.
