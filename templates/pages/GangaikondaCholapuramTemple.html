Gangaikonda Cholapuram Temple – Tamil Nadu

Gangaikonda Cholapuram Temple is a magnificent temple located in Tamil Nadu and built by the Chola king Rajendra Chola I.

It is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is famous for its grand architecture and majestic vimana.

The temple is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and an outstanding example of Chola temple architecture.

Historically, Gangaikonda Cholapuram was the capital city of the Chola dynasty.

With its rich history and architectural beauty, the temple attracts devotees and tourists from across the world.
