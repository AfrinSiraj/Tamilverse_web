Gingee Fort – Villupuram

Gingee Fort, also known as Senji Fort, is one of the most impregnable and historically significant forts in South India.

Located in Villupuram district of Tamil Nadu, the fort complex consists of three hill forts—Rajagiri, Krishnagiri, and Chandrayandurg.

Built and expanded by various dynasties including the Cholas, Vijayanagara rulers, Marathas, and the Nawabs, Gingee Fort reflects a rich military and architectural heritage.

Often called the “Troy of the East”, the fort offers breathtaking views and stands as a symbol of strategic brilliance.
