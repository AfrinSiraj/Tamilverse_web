Government Arts College Museum – Kumbakonam

Government Arts College Museum is a cultural and historical museum located in Kumbakonam.

The museum showcases ancient artifacts, sculptures, and historical collections.

It provides insight into the region’s heritage, art, and traditions.

Students and visitors can explore valuable exhibits related to history and archaeology.

It is a must-visit place for those interested in culture and education.
