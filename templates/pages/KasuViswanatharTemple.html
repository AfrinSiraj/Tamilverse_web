Kasu Viswanathar Temple – Kumbakonam

Kasu Viswanathar Temple is a sacred Shiva temple located in Kumbakonam, Tamil Nadu.

It is dedicated to Lord Shiva as Viswanathar and is known for its spiritual importance.

The temple is associated with the famous Mahamaham festival and attracts many devotees.

It features traditional South Indian temple architecture and serene surroundings.

Devotees visit this temple seeking blessings for prosperity and well-being.
