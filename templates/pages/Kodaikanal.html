Kodaikanal – Dindigul

Kodaikanal is a beautiful hill station located in the Dindigul district of Tamil Nadu.

It is known for its cool climate, misty hills, and scenic landscapes.

Popular attractions include Kodaikanal Lake, Coaker’s Walk, and Pillar Rocks.

The place is famous for its peaceful environment and natural beauty.

Kodaikanal is one of the most loved tourist destinations in South India.
