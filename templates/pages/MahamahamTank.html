Mahamaham Tank – Kumbakonam

Mahamaham Tank is a sacred temple tank located in Kumbakonam, Tamil Nadu.

It is famous for the Mahamaham Festival, which takes place once every 12 years and attracts millions of pilgrims from across India.

Devotees believe that taking a holy dip in the tank washes away sins and brings spiritual blessings.

Surrounded by several ancient temples, the tank serves as an important religious and cultural landmark.

Mahamaham Tank is often called the “Kashi of the South” because of its deep spiritual significance.
