Ooty (Udhagamandalam) – Nilgiris

Ooty, also known as Udhagamandalam, is a beautiful hill station located in the Nilgiri Hills of Tamil Nadu.

It is famous for its cool climate, scenic landscapes, and lush green tea plantations.

The town offers attractions like Ooty Lake, Botanical Gardens, and the Nilgiri Mountain Railway.

It is one of the most popular tourist destinations in South India.

With its natural beauty and peaceful atmosphere, Ooty is perfect for relaxation and travel.
