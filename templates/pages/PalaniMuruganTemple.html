Palani Murugan Temple – Palani

Palani Murugan Temple is one of the most famous temples dedicated to Lord Murugan, located in Palani, Tamil Nadu.

The temple is situated on the Palani Hills and is one of the six sacred abodes (Arupadai Veedu) of Lord Murugan.

It is known for its spiritual significance, scenic location, and the unique idol made of Navapashanam.

Devotees reach the temple by steps, ropeway, or winch, making the pilgrimage a special experience.

With its divine atmosphere and cultural importance, the Palani Murugan Temple attracts millions of devotees every year.
