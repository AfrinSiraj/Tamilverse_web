Potramarai Kulam – Kumbakonam

Potramarai Kulam is a sacred temple tank located in Kumbakonam, Tamil Nadu.

It is closely associated with the famous temples in the town and holds religious importance.

The tank is known for its calm atmosphere and spiritual significance.

Devotees often visit this place for rituals and peaceful reflection.

It is an important part of Kumbakonam’s rich temple heritage.
