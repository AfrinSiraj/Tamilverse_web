Ramanathaswamy Temple – Rameswaram

Ramanathaswamy Temple is a famous and sacred temple located in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu.

It is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is one of the twelve Jyotirlinga temples in India.

The temple is well known for its long corridors, beautiful pillars, and sacred water tanks (theerthams).

It is also one of the Char Dham pilgrimage sites, making it an important spiritual destination.

With its rich history and religious significance, the Ramanathaswamy Temple attracts pilgrims from across the country.
