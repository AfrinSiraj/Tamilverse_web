Ramaswamy Temple – Kumbakonam

Ramaswamy Temple is located in Kumbakonam, Tamil Nadu, and is one of the important Vishnu temples in the region.

The temple was built in the 16th century CE during the Thanjavur Nayak period and is dedicated to Lord Rama.

The walls of the praakarams beautifully depict scenes from the Ramayanam, showcasing remarkable artistic and cultural heritage.

The temple was initiated by Achuthappa Nayak and completed during the reign of Raghunatha Nayak, and it is also one of the five prominent Vishnu temples associated with the Mahamaham festival held once every twelve years.
