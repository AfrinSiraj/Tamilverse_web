Sankagiri Fort – Salem

Sankagiri Fort is a historic hill fort located in Salem district of Tamil Nadu. It stands atop Sankagiri hill and played a key role in South Indian military history.

The fort was used by several rulers including the Vijayanagara Empire, Madurai Nayaks, Hyder Ali, Tipu Sultan, and later the British.

Known for its strong ramparts, multiple gateways, granaries, and watch towers, Sankagiri Fort served as an important strategic defense point.

Today, the fort is a protected monument and offers panoramic views of the surrounding Salem region.
