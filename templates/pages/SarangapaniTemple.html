Sarangapani Temple – Kumbakonam

Sarangapani Temple is one of the most famous ancient temples in Kumbakonam, Tamil Nadu.

It is dedicated to Lord Sarangapani, a form of Lord Vishnu, worshipped in a majestic chariot posture.

The temple is renowned for its magnificent 173-foot Rajagopuram, one of the tallest temple towers in South India.

It is one of the sacred 108 Divya Desams praised by the Alwars, the Vaishnava poet-saints.

With its rich history, beautiful architecture, and spiritual significance, the Sarangapani Temple attracts thousands of devotees and tourists every year.
