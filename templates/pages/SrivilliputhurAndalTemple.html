Srivilliputhur Andal Temple – Virudhunagar

Srivilliputhur Andal Temple is a famous temple located in Srivilliputhur, Tamil Nadu.

It is dedicated to Goddess Andal, the only female Alvar saint, and Lord Vishnu.

The temple is well known for its towering Rajagopuram, which is also the official emblem of the Tamil Nadu Government.

It is one of the 108 Divya Desams and holds great importance in Vaishnavism.

With its rich history, beautiful architecture, and spiritual significance, the temple attracts devotees from all over India.
