St. Thomas Mount Church – Chennai

St. Thomas Mount Church is one of the most important Christian pilgrimage sites in Tamil Nadu.

This sacred hilltop church is believed to be the place where Saint Thomas the Apostle was martyred in 72 AD.

The location offers a calm spiritual atmosphere along with breathtaking panoramic views of Chennai city.

The site stands as a symbol of faith, peace, and historical significance.
