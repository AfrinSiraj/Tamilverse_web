Thanjavur Maratha Palace – Thanjavur

Thanjavur Maratha Palace is a historic royal complex located in the heart of Thanjavur city, Tamil Nadu. It served as the residence of the Maratha rulers who governed Thanjavur from the 17th century onwards.

The palace complex includes several notable structures such as the Durbar Hall, Queen’s Courtyard, and the Saraswathi Mahal Library, one of the oldest libraries in Asia.

Known for its blend of Maratha, Nayak, and European architectural elements, the palace reflects the cultural richness and political history of the Thanjavur region.

Today, the palace is a major tourist attraction and houses museums that showcase royal artifacts, paintings, manuscripts, and weapons.
