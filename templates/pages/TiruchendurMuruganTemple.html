Tiruchendur Murugan Temple – Tiruchendur

Tiruchendur Murugan Temple is a famous temple located in Tiruchendur, Tamil Nadu.

It is dedicated to Lord Murugan and is one of the six sacred abodes (Arupadai Veedu) of Lord Murugan.

The temple is uniquely situated on the shore of the Bay of Bengal, making it one of the few seaside temples in India.

It is known for its spiritual importance, beautiful architecture, and the annual Skanda Sashti festival.

With its divine atmosphere and scenic coastal location, the Tiruchendur Murugan Temple attracts thousands of devotees and tourists every year.
