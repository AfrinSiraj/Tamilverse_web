Vattakottai Fort – Kanyakumari

Vattakottai Fort is a coastal fort located near Kanyakumari, Tamil Nadu. Built during the Travancore kingdom, it stands close to the Arabian Sea with stunning ocean views.

The fort was constructed in the 18th century using granite stones and served as a coastal defense structure protecting the region from naval attacks.

Surrounded by sea on one side and hills on the other, Vattakottai Fort offers a unique blend of military architecture and natural beauty.

Today, the fort is maintained by the Archaeological Survey of India and is a popular tourist destination in Kanyakumari district.
