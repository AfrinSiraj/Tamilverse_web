Yercaud – Salem

Yercaud is a peaceful hill station located in the Shevaroy Hills of Salem district, Tamil Nadu.

It is known for its cool climate, coffee plantations, and scenic viewpoints.

Popular attractions include Yercaud Lake, Pagoda Point, and Botanical Garden.

It is a budget-friendly and less crowded alternative to Ooty and Kodaikanal.

Yercaud is perfect for nature lovers and a relaxing getaway.
